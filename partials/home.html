Why should you care?

Of the top 10 leading causes of death, Alzheimer’s is the 6th and the only one that cannot be slowed, prevented or cured.

Total cost of care for those with Alzheimer’s.

More than 5 million people are living with Alzheimer's Disease in the U.S. This number is predicted to reach up to 16 million by 2050.

Nearly two thirds of Americans with Alzheimers are women.

Where are we now?

Each chapter of our national network seeks to cure Alzheimer's through various efforts. Starting a chapter at your high school or college is a grassroots, high-impact first step toward inspiring your community to end this disease. Every chapter receives personalized advice from our volunteer staff to help it grow and webspace to promote their efforts. Check out our founding chapter!
